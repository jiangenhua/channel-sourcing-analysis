🟦 Engagement %
(SUM(like) + SUM(comment)) / SUM(play) × 100
综合互动率, 衡量内容粘性. 2%+ 健康, 5%+ 优质, <1% 大水号.
🟪 Likes / 100 plays
SUM(like) / SUM(play) × 100
每 100 次播放贡献几次点赞. YouTube 一般 1~5, 爆款娱乐/喜剧能到 5~10.
🟥 Comments / 1K plays
SUM(comment) / SUM(play) × 1000
用户讨论度. 一般 0.5~5, News/Politics 可达 5~20, 宗教/争议刷量 > 30.
阅读方法: 颜色越亮 (蓝→紫→粉) 表示该类目在该指标上数值越高. Nonprofits 和 People&Blogs 在 Comments/1K 列异常突出 (>40) 是宗教布道/争议话题刷评论的典型特征, 需要在质量筛选时单独识别为噪声; 而 Education / News / Science / Howto / Comedy 在三个维度上分布均衡, 是真正"高互动 + 高粘性"的优质类目.

质量分公式

QualityScore(channel) = Σ wi × band(mi)    ,  band ∈ {S:100, A:75, B:50, C:25}
mi: 第 i 个维度的原始测量值 (如 1080p 占比、视频数等)
band(mi): 按维度阈值表把数值落到 S/A/B/C 四档
wi: 各维度权重, ∑wi = 1.0 (100%)
缺失数据: 按 C 级(25 分) 计入, 避免空值惩罚过重
总分映射 ➝ 分级: S 级 ≥ 80 A 级 60-80 B 级 40-60 C 级 < 40

Channel 规模 vs 总播放 (精品 vs 大水号)

精品 (Elite) 大水号 (Bulk)
🌟 精品 channel (Elite) 判定规则
elite ⇔
  quality_score ≥ 65
  AND video_cnt ≤ 5000
  AND (res_1080p ≥ 80% 或 NULL)
  AND (dur_60s ≥ 60% 或 NULL)
即"少而精": 视频量适中, 高清+长视频比例双高, 综合得分合格.
⚠️ 大水号 (Bulk) 判定规则
bulk ⇔
  video_cnt > 5000
  OR quality_score < 50
  OR dur_60s < 30%
即"高频低质": 视频量极大但单条质量低 (典型: 新闻台、TV 频道、Shorts 工厂).
▸ 双对数轴: 横轴 = 单 channel 视频数 (log), 纵轴 = 总播放 (log). 左上区域 为优选 channel (少视频 + 高总播放); 右下区域 为大水号 (多视频但平均播放低).

数据量 / channel 数 双视角

关键 SQL 落地

WITH channel_stats AS (
  SELECT
    author,
    unified_category,
    COUNT(*) AS video_cnt,
    SUM(play_num) AS total_play,
    AVG(CAST(width>=1920 AS INT))*100
      AS res_1080p_pct,
    AVG(CAST(duration>=60 AS INT))*100
      AS dur_60s_pct,
    (SUM(like_num)+SUM(comment_num))*100.0
      / NULLIF(SUM(play_num),0)
      AS engagement_pct,
    MAX(channel_follower_count) AS follower
  FROM base
  GROUP BY author, unified_category
)
SELECT *
FROM channel_stats
WHERE video_cnt >= 50
  AND res_1080p_pct >= 60
  AND dur_60s_pct  >= 50
  AND engagement_pct >= 2
  AND follower >= 10000
  AND unified_category IN (
    'Travel & Events',
    'Education',
    'Sports',
    'Autos & Vehicles',
    'Pets & Animals',
    'Howto & Style',
    'Science & Technology',
    'Food',
    'Family'
  )
ORDER BY total_play DESC;
